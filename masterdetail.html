    I See your Silhouette

    I see your silhouette,
    An artist in a museum,
    Hundreds of creatives surround,
    But my eyes still land on you

    Paint my back like a canvass,
    Soften my heart like paint in water,
    For for every stroke,
    I am filled with color

    I see your silhouette,
    A writer in his comfort,
    Hundreds of papers surround,
    But you chose this one poem

    Write over me your secrets,
    Your wishes and unpleasant memories,
    And though I'll bleed in red ink,
    I'll love you even so

    -V
